Order Policy

Deposit Policy

To ensure a smooth and efficient order process, we require a 30% deposit for every order. This deposit confirms your commitment and enables us to reserve resources and allocate time for its completion.

Purpose of Deposit

The deposit serves as a guarantee that you will follow through with the order, allowing us to:

  • Reserve production time and resources
  • Prioritize your order in our workflow
  • Begin preparing materials for your order

Deposit Details

  • Amount: 30% of total order value
  • Payment methods: Zelle, CashApp, and Cash
  • Due: Upon placement of order

Balance Payment

The remaining balance (70% of total order value) is due when your order is completed. Failure to send payment will result in not receiving your order.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

If you cancel your order, the deposit is non-refundable.

⚠ All bookings must be confirmed within 24 hours of placement.
